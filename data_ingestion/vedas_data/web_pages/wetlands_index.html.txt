Aphid population is above ETL, farmers are advised to spray Thiamethoxam 25% WG @ 1
									g / l water (around 500-1000 l water/ ha; 15 ml per 15 l sprayer tank).
Add 1 spoon full of washing powder or any sticker in the solution before spraying
									per 15 lit tank.
Spraying should be done in the afternoon for avoidance of toxicity to pollinators.
